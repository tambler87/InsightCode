Website Accessibility Standards (AA Rating)

This website follows the 13 key accessibility guidelines for achieving a WCAG AA rating:

  • Provide text alternatives for non-text content (like alt text for images).
  • Ensure adaptable content that works across devices.
  • Provide alternatives for time-based media (e.g., captions for audio).
  • Make content easy to see and hear, with adjustable contrast and volume controls.
